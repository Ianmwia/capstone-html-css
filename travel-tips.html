Travel Tips

  • Plan Ahead: Research your destination, book flights and accommodations early, and create a flexible itinerary to maximize your trip.
  • Pack Smart: Bring versatile clothing, essential medications, travel documents, and chargers. Don’t overpack to stay comfortable and mobile.
  • Stay Safe: Keep copies of important documents, stay aware of your surroundings, and follow local guidelines and advisories.
  • Travel Insurance: Always consider getting travel insurance to cover unexpected events like cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.
  • Local Culture: Respect local customs and traditions, learn a few basic phrases, and try local foods to enrich your experience.
  • Money Matters: Notify your bank before traveling, carry some local currency, and use credit cards wisely to avoid fraud.

FAQs

Q: How do I book a tour with Discovery Tour?
A: You can book directly on our website by selecting your preferred tour and following the checkout process. Alternatively, contact our support team for assistance.
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations made 14 days before the trip are eligible for a full refund. For cancellations after that, please check our detailed policy on the booking page.
Q: Are flights included in your tour packages?
A: Most of our tour packages exclude flights to give you flexibility. However, some all-inclusive packages do include flights—please check the tour details.
Q: Do I need travel insurance?
A: We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances during your trip.
Q: Can I customize my tour itinerary?
A: Yes! We offer customizable tour options. Reach out to our team to tailor your travel plans according to your preferences.
