Travel Tips

  • Plan Ahead: Research your destination, book flights and accommodations early, and create a flexible itinerary to maximize your trip.
  • Pack Smart: Bring versatile clothing, essential medications, travel documents, and chargers. Don’t overpack to stay comfortable and mobile.
  • Stay Safe: Keep copies of important documents, stay aware of your surroundings, and follow local guidelines and advisories.
  • Travel Insurance: Always consider getting travel insurance to cover unexpected events like cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.
  • Local Culture: Respect local customs and traditions, learn a few basic phrases, and try local foods to enrich your experience.
  • Money Matters: Notify your bank before traveling, carry some local currency, and use credit cards wisely to avoid fraud.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour with Discovery Tour?

You can book directly on our website or contact our support team for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations 14+ days in advance are fully refunded. Please refer to the booking page for full details.

Are flights included in your tour packages?

Most of our packages exclude flights for flexibility. Some all-inclusive tours include them—check each tour page.

Do I need travel insurance?

We strongly recommend travel insurance to protect against unforeseen issues during your trip.

Can I customize my tour itinerary?

Yes! Contact our travel consultants to create a personalized itinerary tailored to your preferences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for group bookings.

Are your tours suitable for solo travelers?

Absolutely! Many of our travelers join solo. We also offer shared accommodation options to keep costs low.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 5 or more. Contact our team for a customized quote.

Are your tours child-friendly?

Some tours are family-friendly and suitable for children, while others are geared toward adults. Check the tour description or contact us.

What languages are your tours available in?

Most tours are led in English, but we offer multilingual guides for select destinations. Let us know your preference when booking.

Will I need a visa for my destination?

Visa requirements vary by nationality and destination. Please check with your local embassy or contact us for guidance.

What is included in the tour price?

Tour inclusions vary but usually cover accommodation, transport during the tour, guide services, and some meals. See the specific tour page for details.

What should I pack for my tour?

Packing lists are provided for each destination. Generally, bring comfortable clothing, good walking shoes, a valid ID/passport, and weather-appropriate gear.

How fit do I need to be to join a tour?

Most tours are moderate in activity, but some involve hiking or extended walking. Fitness requirements are listed in each tour description.

Can I change my booking after confirmation?

Changes are possible depending on availability and the time before departure. Contact us as early as possible to make modifications.
