Mount Longonot

Mount Longonot

Mount Longonot is a dormant stratovolcano located in Kenya's Great Rift Valley. Famous for its dramatic caldera and steep slopes, it offers one of the most rewarding hiking experiences in the country. The climb to the summit provides breathtaking panoramic views of the Rift Valley and the crater lake nestled within the caldera.

Activities

  • Hike to the summit via scenic trails
  • Explore the volcanic crater and its lake
  • Spot diverse wildlife including baboons and bird species
  • Enjoy stunning photography opportunities at sunrise and sunset
  • Camping in designated areas within the national park
