Nairobi National Park

Nairobi National Park is a unique wildlife reserve located just outside Kenya’s capital city. It offers visitors the rare opportunity to experience a natural safari with stunning views of Nairobi’s skyline in the background. The park is home to a wide range of wildlife, including lions, rhinos, giraffes, and over 400 bird species.

Highlights

  • See the Big Five animals in a protected environment
  • Enjoy game drives and guided walking safaris
  • Visit the Nairobi Safari Walk for educational exhibits
  • Birdwatch at diverse habitats including wetlands and grasslands
  • Photograph wildlife against the city skyline

Visitor Activities

Visitors to Nairobi National Park can enjoy a variety of activities that combine adventure and education. From early morning game drives to nature walks and picnic spots, the park offers experiences suitable for families, photographers, and wildlife enthusiasts.

Activities

  • Guided walking safaris with expert rangers
  • Picnic in designated scenic areas
  • Visit the Animal Orphanage within the park
  • Attend educational programs and conservation talks
  • Enjoy sunset views and evening drives
