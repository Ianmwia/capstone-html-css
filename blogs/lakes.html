Great Lakes Of Rift Valley

Lake Bogoria

Lake Bogoria is a shallow, saline lake famous for its natural hot springs, geysers, and huge flocks of lesser flamingoes. Located in the Rift Valley, it offers one of the most surreal and colorful bird-watching experiences in Kenya. The geothermal activity around the lake makes it a must-see for nature lovers and photographers.

Activities

  • Watch thousands of flamingoes in their natural habitat
  • Explore steaming hot springs and geysers
  • Take guided nature walks in the surrounding reserve
  • Capture stunning landscapes and birdlife
  • Visit nearby cultural sites of the Endorois community

Lake Nakuru

Located inside Lake Nakuru National Park, this lake is a prime safari destination. It's surrounded by acacia forests and home to flamingoes, rhinos, giraffes, lions, and over 400 bird species. Lake Nakuru is ideal for wildlife viewing and eco-tourism in a protected environment.

Activities

  • Go on a game drive to see rhinos, lions, and more
  • Watch flamingoes and pelicans feeding in the lake
  • Picnic at scenic viewpoints like Baboon Cliff
  • Visit Makalia Falls inside the park
  • Enjoy birdwatching in a UNESCO-listed ecosystem
