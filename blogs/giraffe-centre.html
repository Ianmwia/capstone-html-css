Giraffe Centre

Giraffe Centre Nairobi

The Giraffe Centre in Nairobi is a unique conservation and education center dedicated to the endangered Rothschild's giraffe. Visitors get the rare chance to feed and interact closely with these gentle giants, learning about giraffe conservation efforts and the importance of wildlife protection in Kenya.

Highlights

  • Hand-feed Rothschild's giraffes from a raised platform
  • Learn about giraffe conservation and breeding programs
  • Explore nature trails within the center's grounds
  • Visit the informative giraffe museum
  • Enjoy family-friendly and educational experiences

Visitor Experiences

Beyond feeding giraffes, visitors can engage with knowledgeable guides, take memorable photos, and participate in workshops focused on wildlife conservation. The Giraffe Centre also supports local community projects, making it a meaningful stop for tourists interested in sustainable tourism.

Activities

  • Guided tours explaining giraffe behavior and ecology
  • Photography sessions with giraffes in a natural setting
  • Educational talks and interactive workshops
  • Supporting community and conservation initiatives
  • Shopping for unique giraffe-themed souvenirs
